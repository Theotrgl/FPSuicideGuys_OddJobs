Odd Jobs Partner

ABOUT US

Nowadays as the growth of technology gets faster and faster more people in this world are getting eaten by their ego and is growing a seed of individualism in their hearts. Odd Jobs is the organization that is fighting against that.

SMILES

A single smile in a persons day can make their day better, whether their pet just died or just won the lottery, A Smile Makes a Difference. Our dream as a company is to participate in making this world full of smiles, motivating others to help a hand in need and achieve success by doing so.

SERVICES

We have a wide range of availability, we will help with any type of problem, requests, tasks, and others as long as it doesn't involve crime or violence. Our services range from heavy duty work and gardening to help find a lost dog or take care of the baby at home.

TRUSTWORTHINESS

Our company is filled with profesionals and veterans at their own sectors and all of our workers have been interviewed for their motives for work through a lie detector test. This is a necessary precaution to take because above our desire to be a successful company, your safety and satisfaction comes first. So please trust in our workers.

NEED FOR FUNDING

You might ask "Why try to get paid if your goal is to solely help others?" Well by earning money not only do we get bigger in name so that more people will be aware of our purpose, but also by earning money we can do better than before, we can start providing facilities like cars and machines to make your job request go smoother, that's our reason as to why we need to earn money. All of it is for achieving our dream.
